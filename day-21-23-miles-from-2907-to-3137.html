Day 21 - 23 miles from 290.7 to 313.7

The hike out of camp this morning was nice and easy as we followed Holcombe Creek for a bit. We climbed out of the sparsely forested boulder field into chaparral hillsides and eventually came upon Deep Creek.

We stopped for lunch here and refilled our water. Hikers slowly collected along the stream and we all excitedly discussed our plans for the hot springs later in the day. We all planned to spend multiple hours there.

We spent about an hour and a half at the creek and worked up the energy to climb our way out. We followed more uncovered chaparral hillside and walked over a few wooden bridges. Eventually we found Bird resting about two miles outside of the hot springs and joined her in the shade.

We hiked into the hot springs together and quickly got in. Multiple hot pools of various temperatures surrounded the main swimming hole. The pools were a huge hiker hangout and we spent two hours here. Finally we hiked out with Bird and Flacco up and through the canyon.

The canyon exited at the bottom of the Mojave River Forks Dam. We had to cross the last trickles of Deep Creek to find flat ground for camp. Frankenstein joined us and we all ate dinner together before falling asleep. Big miles and multiple long breaks left us all exhausted.
