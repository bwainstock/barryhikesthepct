Day 22 - 24.6 miles from 369.4 to Buckhorn Campground (Mile 394)

. Steve cooked us a delicious breakfast this morning and drove us to trail early. Frankenstein wasn't hiking out with us because his knee was hurting him so we all said goodbye. The mountains were all covered with snow from the storm.

Because of the heavy snow, we took the High Desert Alternate Route around Mt. Baden Powell instead of going up and over. The trail skirted around the base of the mountain and dropped into the South Fork Campground where we ate lunch. Besides the wind, the weather was wonderful and even slightly warm.

From there we climbed up to the end of the Baden Powell trail over a trail made of sharp granite rocks which fell away from the mountain as we walked over. By the top of the climb the ground was once again covered in snow and we were hiking through thick fog.

It was about six o'clock by the time we got to the top of the trail and we still had about six miles to walk. Because it was so cold and wet we jumped in the back of a trail angel's truck and hitched into Buckhorn Campground. After making a fire and eating dinner we crawled into our tents to warm up and fell asleep.
