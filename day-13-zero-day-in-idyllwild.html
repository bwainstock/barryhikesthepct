Day 13 - Zero Day in Idyllwild

Last night we slept on the lawn of the Paradise Valley Cafe. This meant breakfast was only a few steps away. After wolfing down a huge breakfast burrito, Matt and I wandered over to the highway and stuck our thumbs out for a hitch. After only a few minutes, Blake from Temecula picked us and gave us a tour of Idyllwild. He was visiting his mother for the weekend and invited us over for drinks. Unfortunately we had too many errands to run and thanked him for the ride.

The day mostly consisted of resting in our hotel room, hanging out with other hikers, and eating. Because it was a weekend, Idyllwild was swarming with people. Hikers and bikers and Am Diego tourists were everywhere.

At night we went out to dinner with a group of hikers including Hands, Pandora, Salt Lick, Purple Princess, and a few others. Tomorrow we'll get to the post office to pick up some resupply packages, top off our food bag at the market, and climb Devil's Slide Trail out of town.
